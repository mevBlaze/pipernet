# Code of Conduct

We use the Contributor Covenant 2.1 as the baseline.

The short version: treat people well, don't harass anyone, don't be a
bully. The protocol is for everyone including people who aren't like you.

---

## The standard

Examples of behavior that contributes to a good environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Respecting different opinions and experiences
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility when you've made a mistake
- Focusing on what's best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments of any kind
- Publishing someone's private information without their consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions
- Any other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate

---

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, PRs, discussions,
and any community channel affiliated with this repo.

---

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the
maintainers. Reports go to the same contact in `SECURITY.md` (until a
dedicated address exists). We will review and respond. Confidentiality
will be respected.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that
violate this code, and to temporarily or permanently ban contributors
who engage in unacceptable behavior.
